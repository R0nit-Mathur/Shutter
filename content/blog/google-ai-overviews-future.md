Google's AI Overviews have rolled out globally, changing how search results are presented. When users search for queries, Google's Gemini-powered system displays a generated summary at the top, showing cards with links to sources.

## Adapting to Google's Retrieval Model

To be included in Google AI Overviews, your site needs both standard SEO ranking authority and AEO clarity.
- **Provide Direct Answers:** Start articles with a direct, concise definition.
- **Use FAQ Accordions:** Structure your pages to contain Q&A formats matching user queries.
- **Maintain Schema Integrity:** Use FAQ and Article schemas to highlight key concepts.
