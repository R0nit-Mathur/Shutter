Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the next evolution of search engine optimization. As users shift from typing fragments into Google to asking full conversational queries to AI assistants like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini, the search experience changes completely.

Traditional SEO targets listing links. AEO targets the direct, synthesized answer.

## Key Differences Between SEO and AEO

- **SEO:** Optimizes for keywords, page loading speed, and link networks to rank on a results page.
- **AEO:** Optimizes for semantic context, clean factual definitions, structured databases, and authority grounding to be synthesized directly in answer text.

> [!NOTE]
> To win at AEO, your content must be clear, concise, and structured so LLM parser agents can ingest and verify your claims easily.
