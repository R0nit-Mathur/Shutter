AI SEO is the practice of utilizing artificial intelligence tools and machine learning algorithms to automate, scale, and optimize search engine marketing strategies.

AI SEO comprises two key domains:
1. **AI-Assisted Optimization:** Using AI tools to analyze keywords, write content, map sites, and cluster semantic topics for traditional search.
2. **Optimizing for AI Engines (AEO/GEO):** Tuning your website so that generative AI search engines cite and recommend your brand.

## Key Capabilities of AI SEO Tools

AI SEO goes beyond content generation to encompass advanced diagnostics:

* **Semantic Clustering:** Automatically grouping search queries into logical topic hubs.
* **Crawl Budget Analysis:** Predicting spider crawl frequencies based on server response latency.
* **SWOT Brand Audits:** Evaluating how search bots perceive your brand relative to competitors.
* **Citation Indexing:** Mapping citation sources to see which forum, blog, or document references are cited by ChatGPT or Claude.

> [!NOTE]
> Shutter represents the cutting edge of AI SEO, giving brands a diagnostic console to analyze their semantic presence and target citation loops in real-time.
